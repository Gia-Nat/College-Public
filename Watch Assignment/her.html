MK TIME Y

The MK TIME Y watch showcases the most important qualities of Swiss watchmaking. Made of durable and resistant stainless steel, it is accompanied by a Swiss quartz movement with chronograph function. It has a sapphire crystal, up to 7 times harder than mineral glass.

MK TIME II

The MK TIME II impresses with a timeless sporty-elegant design. The MK TIME TIME II, in Silver color, with a pink dial, offers perfect readability thanks to its sapphire crystal.The MK TIME TIME II is a quartz watch with a RONDA 705 movement made in Switzerland, a mechanism of proven quality and precision over the years , indicating the hours, minutes, and seconds with great reliability..

MK TIME DIAMOND

The MK TIME DIAMOND in White color, with a white dial and gold indices, offers perfect readability thanks to its sapphire crystal. the crystal on this watch includes an anti-reflective treatment, greatly preventing the occurrence of uncomfortable reflections that hinder the correct reading of the dial under certain lighting conditions.This watch comes equipped with a golden stainless steel bracelet and a folding clasp, which allows easy and precise adjustment of the strap size, so that the watch fits your wrist in the best possible way.

MK TIME GALAXY

The MK TIME GALAXY draws its inspiration from the shores of the great Scottish lakes. The pebbles found on their beaches have lent it their gentle, round curves. With no angles or edges, our hands are drawn to caress it as it sits gently on the wrist.The generous dial face inspires us to travel, to escape, to be free. On the stone-set version, delicate mother of pearl complements the sparkling diamonds on the case.

MK TIME PEARL

The MK TIME PEARL is more classic then the Galaxy.With a white dial and a general ligher color the MK TIME PEARL is very similar to the gem that inspired it. Comprising a succession of metal pearls, the bracelet is comfortable to wear, whilst accentuating the femininity of the piece.

MK TIME CLASSIC

The MK TIME CLASSIC is a feat of modern craftsmanship, creativity, and design. Showcasing a versatile night blue dial, it also features a brown leather strap. This neutral-coloured piece will keep you chic and on time. The watch is water-resistant to 50m.
