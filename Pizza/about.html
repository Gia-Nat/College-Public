Our Story

Our Restaurant was founded by my grandfather in 1948, since then our family businnes became pizza making. My father grow up making pizza and so did i. After the loss of my grand father, my father runned the restaurant. Our product is made in the traditional neapolitan way, cooked in a wood fired oven.
Our product is made in the traditional neapolitan way, cooked in a wood fired oven. We only use fresh and seasonal ingredients of the highest quality in order to deliver only the best products
All our ingredients comes from Italy and prepared fresh on a daily basis. Our dough is handmade daily and we let it rise for 24h in order to increase his digestibility.
